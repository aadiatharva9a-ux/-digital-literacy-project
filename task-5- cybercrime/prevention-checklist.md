Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist for College Students
•	Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank or customer care. 
•	Do not scan unknown QR codes or click suspicious payment links, especially when someone says you will receive money. 
•	Use strong passwords that include letters, numbers, and special characters, and change them regularly. 
•	Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts such as email, banking apps, and social media. 
•	Check website links carefully before entering personal information. Make sure the website address starts with https:// and looks genuine. 
•	Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online banking or making payments, as it may not be secure. 
•	Install apps only from trusted sources like the Google Play Store or Apple App Store to avoid malware and fake applications. 
•	Keep your phone and computer updated with the latest software and antivirus protection. 
•	Log out of accounts after using shared or public computers, such as those in libraries or computer labs. 
•	Report cybercrime immediately if you become a victim or notice suspicious activity.
