Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

Do's (Things You Should Do)
•	Think before posting and make sure your content is respectful and appropriate. 
•	Protect your personal information, such as phone number, address, and passwords. 
•	Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts and profile. 
•	Share useful and positive content that reflects your personality and values. 
•	Use social media responsibly for learning, networking, and communication. 
________________________________________
Don'ts (Things You Should Avoid)
•	Do not share false or misleading information without checking the source. 
•	Do not post offensive or harmful comments about others. 
•	Do not accept friend requests from strangers you do not know. 
•	Do not spend too much time on social media, especially during study hours. 
•	Do not share confidential or sensitive information online.
